Privacy Policy

PIPO TikTok Studio is a private creator workflow used to upload original videos to an authorized TikTok account.

Data collected

The app stores TikTok OAuth tokens locally on the user's computer only after the user authorizes the app. It does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties.

Data use

Tokens are used only to upload videos through TikTok's official Content Posting API for the authorized account.

Content

The workflow is intended for original videos created locally, including narration, captions, subtitles, and generated visuals.

Contact

Contact the app owner through the TikTok developer account that owns this app.
